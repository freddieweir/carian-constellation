🚀 Next Steps - Deploy Your Platform!

You now have complete, production-ready Kubernetes manifests. Here's how to deploy:

Option A: Full Deployment (Recommended)

  • Step 1: Deploy Terraform infrastructure (if not already done)
  • Step 2: Create secrets: cd kubernetes/secrets && ./create-secrets.sh
  • Step 3: Deploy namespaces: kubectl apply -f namespaces/
  • Step 4: Deploy secrets: kubectl apply -f secrets/external-secrets.yaml
  • Step 5: Deploy database: kubectl apply -f applications/postgresql/
  • Step 6: Deploy applications: kubectl apply -f applications/
  • Step 7: Configure & deploy ingress: kubectl apply -f ingress/
  • Step 8: Deploy monitoring: kubectl apply -f monitoring/

Option B: Incremental Deployment

  • Deploy services one at a time
  • Test each component before moving to the next
  • Follow the detailed README.md in kubernetes/ directory

Option C: Review & Customize

  • Review all manifests for your specific requirements
  • Adjust resource limits, replica counts, storage sizes
  • Customize ingress domains and SSL certificates
